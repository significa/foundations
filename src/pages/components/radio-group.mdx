# RadioGroup

A group of checkable buttons allowing only one option to be selected at a time.

<Box>
  <RadioGroup defaultValue="rg2" className="text-sm text-primary">
    <div className="flex gap-2 items-center">
      <RadioGroupItem id="rg1" value="rg1" />
      <label htmlFor="rg1">Boiled</label>
    </div>
    <div className="flex gap-2 items-center">
      <RadioGroupItem id="rg2" value="rg2" />
      <label htmlFor="rg2">Fried</label>
    </div>
    <div className="flex gap-2 items-center">
      <RadioGroupItem id="rg3" value="rg3" />
      <label htmlFor="rg3">Scrambled</label>
    </div>
  </RadioGroup>
</Box>
