Blocks are full sections composed from Foundations primitives — copy the whole thing,
then swap the markup and wire up your own submit handler. This sign-in card pulls
together [Field](/ui/field), [Input](/ui/input), [Button](/ui/button),
[Checkbox](/ui/checkbox), and [Divider](/ui/divider).

It is intentionally presentational: there's no auth logic, no form library. Bring your
own — `react-hook-form` + Zod pairs well with [Field](/ui/field)'s `invalid` prop.

## Best Practices

1. **Validation**: drive [Field](/ui/field)'s `invalid` prop from your validation source and surface errors with `Field.Error`.
2. **Submission**: handle `onSubmit` and set the submit button's `isLoading` while the request is in flight.
3. **Accessibility**: keep the `autoComplete` hints (`email`, `current-password`) so password managers work.
