## Tier 3 â€” CI validation of `page.mdx` deps

- Convention: declare deps in `page.mdx` frontmatter:
  ```yaml
  dependencies:
    - components/slot
    - ui/popover
    - ui/divider
  ```
- Script:
  - Walk `src/foundations/<cat>/<name>/<name>.tsx`
  - Extract `@/foundations/...` imports
  - Skip utilities defined in `setup.mdx` (parse `setup.mdx` itself to get the canonical list â€” one source of truth)
  - Compare to frontmatter `dependencies`
  - Fail if drift
